Chandigarh offers world-class wedding venues including JW Marriott Hotel Chandigarh, Taj Chandigarh, The Lalit Chandigarh, Hyatt Centric Sector 17, and Hotel Mountview. Decor By Pearls has worked at all these venues and can help you select the best fit.

Absolutely. We cover the entire Tricity area — Chandigarh, Mohali, and Zirakpur — and work with venues like Radisson RED Mohali, Holiday Inn Zirakpur, The Mohali Club, and Park Plaza Zirakpur.

What is the cost of planning a 5-star wedding in Chandigarh?

Wedding costs vary based on the venue, guest count, décor preferences, and services required. Decor By Pearls offers customised packages for every budget. Contact us for a free consultation and personalised quote.
