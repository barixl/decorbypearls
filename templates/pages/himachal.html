Which are the best wedding venues in Kasauli?

Kasauli's top wedding venues include Ramada by Wyndham Kasauli, Glenview Resort Kasauli, and Suryavilas Luxury Resort & Spa. These hill properties offer breathtaking mountain views and premium facilities perfect for destination weddings.

What is the best time for a wedding in Himachal / Kasauli?

The best months for a Himachal wedding are March–June and September–November, when the weather is pleasant and skies are clear. We recommend avoiding the monsoon season (July–August) for outdoor ceremonies.
